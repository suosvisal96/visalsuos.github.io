# Privacy Policy — Grok Easy Automation

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. All automation runs locally in your browser. Settings are saved only in your own Chrome storage. No analytics, no tracking, no external servers.

## What we do NOT collect
- No personal information
- No browsing history
- No prompts or generated content
- No account data from Grok.com
- No location data
- No payment information

## How the extension works
All automation logic runs entirely inside your Chrome browser. The extension interacts only with grok.com/imagine on your behalf. No data leaves your device to any server we control.

## Chrome Storage
Settings such as delay timers, folder names, and theme preferences are saved using Chrome's built-in storage API (chrome.storage.sync). This data stays in your Google account and is never accessible to us.
